Page Rank

Google page rank- 3/10

Page Rank is a numeric value that represents how sgnificant a page is on the web. Google finds that when one page links to another page, it casts a vote for the other page. More votes that a page accumulates, the more more important it becomes.  Also, the importance of the page that is casting the vote determines how important the vote itself is. Google calculates a page's importance from the votes cast for it. When the page rank for a page is calculated you can determine the importance of the page.
www.domain.com has a page rank 3. A website with page rank 3 or below is not much trusted by search engines. The ideal page rank should be 4 or higher.  Websites with higher Page Rank are considered an authoritative source. High Page Rank can also improve Search Engine Results Page (SERP) performance.

Backlinks are also known as incoming links, inbound links, inlinks, and inward links. The number of backlinks is one indication of the popularity or importance of a website or page. Search engines often use the number of backlinks that a website has as one of the most important factors for determining that website's search engine ranking. Backlinks from authoritative sites, or sites that have a high page rank, on a given topic are highly valuable. If both sites have content geared toward the keyword topic, the backlink is considered relevant and believed to have strong influence on the search engine rankings of the webpage granted the backlink.
